World's Biggest University

Newton University, named after the renowned physicist Sir Isaac Newton, is an esteemed institution that upholds a tradition of excellence in education and research. With a commitment to fostering scientific inquiry, critical thinking, and innovation, Newton University has established itself as a prominent center for learning in the fields of physics, mathematics, and engineering.

Courses We Offer

Newton University offers a wide range of courses across various disciplines to cater to the diverse interests and career aspirations of students. Some of the prominent courses offered at Newton University include:

Physics

A comprehensive program that covers classical mechanics, quantum mechanics, electromagnetism, thermodynamics, and astrophysics, providing students with a deep understanding of the fundamental laws governing the physical world.

Mathematics

An extensive curriculum that explores topics such as calculus, algebra, differential equations, number theory, and mathematical modeling, enabling students to develop strong analytical and problem-solving skills.

Engineering

Specialized engineering programs, including mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, civil engineering, and computer engineering, equipping students with the knowledge and skills required to design, build, and innovate in various technological fields.

Computer Science

An evolving field that offers courses in programming, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and software engineering, preparing students for careers in the rapidly growing tech industry.

Business Administration

A comprehensive business program that covers areas such as finance, marketing, operations, human resources, and entrepreneurship, providing students with a strong foundation in business principles and leadership skills.

Psychology

A program that delves into the study of human behavior, cognition, emotions, and mental processes, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of psychological theories and methodologies.

Our Global Campus

Newton University's global campus offers a vibrant and inclusive community that prepares students to thrive in an interconnected world. Through its diverse student body, international collaborations, and cross-cultural experiences, the university cultivates a global perspective and equips students with the skills and knowledge to navigate the complexities of a globalized society.

Our Facilities

Newton University's facilities provide a conducive and modern learning environment, empowering students to explore, learn, and thrive in their academic pursuits. By ensuring that students have access to cutting-edge resources and infrastructure, the university creates an environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development.

World Class Library

Newton University's libraries are extensive and well-stocked, housing a vast collection of books, journals, research papers, and digital resources. Students have access to a wide range of academic materials, both online and offline, enabling them to conduct in-depth research and stay abreast of the latest developments in their fields.

Largest Play Ground

The playground at Newton University offers a variety of facilities to cater to the recreational needs of students. It features open green spaces for outdoor sports such as soccer, cricket, and ultimate frisbee. There are well-maintained courts for basketball, tennis, and volleyball, allowing students to engage in team sports and friendly competitions.

Tasty and Healthy Food

The cafeteria at Newton University serves as a hub for students to refuel, recharge, and connect with their peers. It not only provides nourishing meals but also creates a welcoming space that fosters a sense of community and well-being on campus.
